What is BreMetCan ?

BreMetCan is a database for studying the effects of human breast cancer metastasis.

Here, we break down the data in terms of substance and scope, making it easier for you to access what you'll find in BreMetCan and how to get the most out of it for your research needs.

Overview

BreMetCan is a database that comprises of data from the COSMIC - Catalogue of Somatic Mutations in cancer, HCMDB (Human Cancer Metastasis Database) and UniprotKB
