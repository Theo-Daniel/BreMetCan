What is Metastatic
Breast Cancer ?

Metastatic Breat Cancer is the most advance stage of breast cancer develops when abnormal cells in the breast starts to divide uncontrollably. Atumor is a mass or collection of these abnormal cells.

Metastasis refers to cancer cells thaat have spread to a new area of the body. In metastic breast cancer, cells may spread to the:

  • Bones
  • Brain
  • Liver
  • Lung
